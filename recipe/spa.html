Spaghetti and Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup dry red wine
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, ground pork, bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, eggs, milk, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Shape the mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.
  4. Place the meatballs on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until cooked through.
  5. While the meatballs are baking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  6. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  7. Add the crushed tomatoes, red wine, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  8. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  9. Add the spaghetti and cook according to package directions.
  10. Drain the spaghetti and return it to the pot.
  11. Add the spaghetti sauce and meatballs to the pot and stir to combine.
  12. Serve immediately with grated Parmesan cheese.
