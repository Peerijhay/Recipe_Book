How to Bake an Apple Cake

Apple cake is a delicious and easy-to-make dessert that is perfect for any occasion. It is also a great way to use up leftover apples.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups peeled, cored, and sliced apples
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  5. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  7. Fold in the apples and walnuts (if using).
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.
  9. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
  10. Let the cake cool completely before serving.

Tips

* For a more flavorful cake, use a variety of apples, such as Granny Smith, Honeycrisp, and Fuji. * To prevent the apples from sinking to the bottom of the cake, toss them in a tablespoon of flour before adding them to the batter. * For a richer flavor, add a teaspoon of cinnamon or nutmeg to the batter. * To make a glaze for the cake, combine 1 cup of powdered sugar, 2 tablespoons of milk, and 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract in a small bowl. Drizzle the glaze over the cooled cake.

Enjoy!
